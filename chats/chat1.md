## Product concept

Design a standalone AI-powered learning platform / LMS for children, teenagers, and modern learners.

The platform combines:
- AI tutoring
- structured courses
- interactive lessons
- learning journeys
- quizzes and challenges
- progress tracking
- gamification
- teacher/admin management
- parent-friendly learning insights

The product should feel like a premium, friendly, AI-first learning environment.

- paperclip character

- friendly face
- notebook / paper learning theme
- playful assistant personality

Do not completely redesign the mascot. Polish it visually so it fits inside a modern premium EdTech UI.

The mascot should appear throughout the app as an interactive AI tutor, not just as decoration.

Create UI moments where the mascot:
- welcomes the learner
- gives hints

Create different mascot interaction states:

- loading / AI is thinking
